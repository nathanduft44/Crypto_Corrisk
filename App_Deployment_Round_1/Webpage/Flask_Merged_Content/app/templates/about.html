About Our Team

We are a group of young professionals who all share a passion for staying informed on events within the cryptocurrency space.
Despite our love for this industry, we recognize the need for more sources of unbiased market information.

In order for the cryptocurrency sector to go mainstream, participants in the space need to improve the general quality of discourse regarding market trends.
As a part of this general improvement, participants in this market will need to integrate traditional metrics for evaluating securties.

We aim to provide our users with a simple and easily understandable platform where they can access quality visuals based on conventionally accepted analytical methods.

Our primary wish is to provide utility for users who already understand traditional security valuation strategies for risk and reward.
However, we also hope to encourage inexperienced speculators to add to their analytical toolbox when evaluating cryptocurrency assets.
